About Fazil Enterprises

A local iron and steel supplier supporting small and large fabrication projects.

Fazil Enterprises is an iron and steel material shop located on Nagariya Road, near Qadariya Madrsa, Uttar Pradesh, India. The shop is owned and managed by Mohd Fazil Ansari.

For more than 6 years, we have been supplying iron pipes, angles, grills, TMT bars and related steel items that are commonly used for gates, window grills, doors, boundary walls and small structural work.

Our customers include local fabricators, contractors, hardware shops and individual homeowners. We try to keep regular material in stock so projects do not stop due to shortage of basic iron and steel sections.

Whether you need material in wholesale quantity or a few pieces for a small job, Fazil Enterprises is ready to serve with genuine rates and practical guidance.
