Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you provide delivery?
Delivery may be possible in nearby areas depending on distance and order size. Please mention your location in the enquiry or call the shop for clarity.
2. Do you handle custom sizes or cutting?
Basic cutting may be arranged as per requirement and availability. Detailed fabrication work is usually done by your fabricator or welder.
3. Is there a minimum order for wholesale?
For wholesale rates, usually a larger quantity is required. You can share your requirement and we will inform you about the best possible rate.
4. Do you provide material for small jobs?
Yes, retail quantity is also available. Even for a single gate, grill or window, you can purchase the required iron and steel sections.
5. Do you give GST invoices?
GST billing may be available depending on the nature of the order. Please discuss at the time of enquiry for billing details.
6. Which areas do you mainly serve?
We primarily serve customers around Nagariya Road and nearby localities, but people from other parts of Bareilly and nearby villages also purchase material from us.
